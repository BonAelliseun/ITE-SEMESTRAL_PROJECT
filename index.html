The Problem

Through our Empathize stage, we identified that communities are suffering from poorly maintained surroundings. This negatively affects safety and health, creating a need for a solution that goes beyond just physical structures.

Our Needs Statement

"To create safer, healthier, and more livable communities by directly addressing the challenges people face every day due to environmental neglect."
